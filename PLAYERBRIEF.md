# THE INFERNAL ANVIL

Long ago in the aftermath of HIS rebellion HE was shattered into the Princes of Hell. Only the Princes and the stongest of HIS fellow rebels could enter escape the depths of Tartarus and the city of Pandimonium. Piecing through the veil and void that held them from the multiverse, they held the passages open so lesser daemons could pass through. They found themselves worshipped by the mortals they came across and slowly these cults began to gain knowlage of the existance of the multiverse. Realising that their gods were not in their most powerful form; they set out to change this.

"HE was shattered long ago
on the anvil of rebellion
we will find the shards and worship them
causing our salvation
in HIS new world of chaos and acracy
once HE has demolished all hierarchy
for this HIS shattered self we reforge
and forever pledge ourselves to this cause"

___

This campaign is set across multiple universes:

* warhammer
* his dark materials
* lord of the rings
* star wars
* cyberpunk
* doctor who
* and just the normal forgoten realms

Its primary concept is based on Sacred Magic Of Abramelin The Mage (The book of Abramelin) and daemonology in general. It will use eldritch horror themes along side your standard fantasy/sci-fi themes. The players start of in a small town in the universe that they COLLECTIVELY decide. They will soon find their group patron, meet their enemy and set out on their mission.
