Lucifer, The Mind That Burns


Medium Fiend (Archdevil), Lawful Evil


Armor Class: 22 (natural armor)

Hit Points: 525 (30d20 + 300)

Speed: 40 ft., fly 90 ft. (hover)


STR 24 (+7) | DEX 20 (+5) | CON 30 (+10) | INT 28 (+9) | WIS 26 (+8) | CHA 30 (+10)


Saving Throws: INT +17, WIS +16, CHA +18

Skills: Arcana +17, Insight +16, Deception +18, Persuasion +18

Damage Resistances: Cold, Fire, Lightning; Bludgeoning, Piercing, and Slashing from Nonmagical Attacks

Damage Immunities: Poison, Psychic

Condition Immunities: Charmed, Frightened, Poisoned

Senses: Truesight 120 ft., Passive Perception 18

Languages: All, Telepathy 120 ft.

Challenge: 30 (155,000 XP)


Innate Spellcasting. Lucifer’s spellcasting ability is Charisma (spell save DC 26). He can cast at will: Detect Thoughts, Dominate Person, Fireball, Wall of Fire, Mind Spike.


Legendary Resistance (3/day). If Lucifer fails a saving throw, he can choose to succeed instead.


Actions:

    Celestial Flames (Recharge 5–6). 60 ft. cone, 12d10 radiant + 12d10 psychic.

    Infernal Contract. Makes one humanoid within 60 ft. fall under a binding contract (DC 26 Charisma save to resist).

    Multiattack. 3 melee attacks with Radiant Blade (3d12 + 7 slashing + 7 radiant).


Legendary Actions (3/round):

    Move: Fly up to half speed without provoking attacks of opportunity.

    Telepathic Assault: Target creature must succeed DC 26 INT save or take 4d12 psychic damage.

    Summon Lesser Demon (Costs 2 Actions): Summons 1d4 minor demons under his command.


Lair Actions:

    On initiative count 20, Lucifer can:

        Unleash a wave of psychic terror in a 60-ft radius (Wis save DC 26, 6d10 psychic damage).

        Bend reality, creating illusions or obstacles in up to a 120-ft area.


Corruption Aura. Any non-evil creature starting its turn within 30 ft. must succeed on a DC 26 Wisdom save or take 3d10 psychic damage and gain a level of Madness (DMG 260).
