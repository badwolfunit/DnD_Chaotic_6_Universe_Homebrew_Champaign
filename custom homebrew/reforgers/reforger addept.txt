Reforger Adept


Medium Humanoid (any), Any Evil Alignment


Armor Class: 15 (leather + magical enhancements)

Hit Points: 85 (10d8 + 40)

Speed: 30 ft.


STR 14 (+2) | DEX 16 (+3) | CON 18 (+4) | INT 16 (+3) | WIS 14 (+2) | CHA 12 (+1)


Skills: Arcana +5, Deception +3, Stealth +5

Saving Throws: INT +5, WIS +4


Actions:

    Demonic Blade: +6 to hit, 2d8+3 slashing + 1d6 fire damage.

    Summon Minor Demon (1/day): Summon 1d2 lesser demons for 1 hour.

    Infernal Ritual: Can perform a 1-hour ritual to corrupt an area or item.

    Dark Patron Boon: Gains a temporary +2 to spell attacks or saves for 1 hour after worship or ritual.
