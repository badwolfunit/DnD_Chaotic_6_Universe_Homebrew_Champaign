# Notes for the map of the reforger base in svalbard:
1. The entrance where the party start. To either side there are small acloves with chests in them with the following:
  * a cookbook
  * an alchemist's notebook
  * a trivet
  * a key (for another chest

2. The main chamber in the dungeon is empty. Oposite the entrance, there is a raised altar accessable by stairs. Behind this there is a statue of Oriens, the demon this temple is dedicated to. At the very back of this area there is a hidden revoling door for priests to enter during worship.

3.  This is a training room with weapon racks around two of the walls. The majority of the area is dedicated to sparing. Within there are two wolves. Seemingly being trained for combat, they, in the seeming abcense of their trainers, are poised for combat.

4. This appears to be the communal dorms. There are five beds layed out on one side. There are three reforgers in the room: two novices, and one addept.

5. This room contains a simple well that appears to be the primary water source for the encampment. There is a light amount of tresure. There is a locked door between this room and room 4 although there is another entrance.

6. At the end of a corridor leading out of room 4 is a small area used to train new recruits and novices. It has a row of benches down one side and a desk at the front. It is mostly safe, just don't get too near to the papers left on the desk (mimic) however interesting they seem.

7. The dungeon prison. It is bisected into a single large filthy cell shared by all that are imprisoned. On the other side there is an altar for sacrifices. On that altar is a palidin (level ~3, member of harpers) with veteran reforger standing over them.

8. This is the quaters of the veteren reforger in room 7. There is a large bed, a writing desk, a altar and in the corner a statue of Oriens. In another corner there is a stack of chests containing the following:
* 5 platinum pieces,
* A Mithral breastplate
* A spare the dying spell scroll
* 7 sets of reforger robes
* 10 reforger amulets
* scrap of paper with the reforger's prophesy:
> HE was shattered long ago
> on the anvil of rebellion
> we will find the shards and worship them
> causing our salvation
> in HIS new world of chaos and acracy
> once HE has demolished all hierarchy
> for this his shattered self we reforge
> and forever pledge ourselves to this cause

On the writing desk there are a few disorganised papers documenting the heirarchy of the princes and daemons, Sanity saving throw to any that read it (dc 10).
